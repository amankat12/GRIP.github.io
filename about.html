About Us

TSF- The sparks Foundation is a not-for profit organization registered in INdia and Singapore and operating globaly. We inspire students to innovate and help them integrate to build a beeter humankind

GRIP- The Graduate Rotational Internship Program is an unique offer for students and recent graduates to experience and join TSF. This program aims to enable students to be professionally capable, and entrepreneurial. Apart from skill specific tasks. we encourage interns to build a credible professional profile.
